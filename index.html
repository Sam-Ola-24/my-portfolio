About Me

I am computer enthusiast with a passion for computer programming, web development and design, and computer animations. I have developed an inatiale interest in computing since childhood, I have a Bachelors degree in Computer Science and I am currently enrolled in the Computer Programming diploma program at Humber College, to gain more knowledge and insight to the world of computer programming.

I have over 5 years of experience in IT, working as Technical Support Analyst, Quality Assurance Engineer, Systems Analyst, and more.
